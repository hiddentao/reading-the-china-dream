This menu groups translated texts according to various scholarly or publication projects with which we are involved. At present there are three:  
  
* **[Rethinking China’s Rise](/rethinking-chinas-rise/).** A series of eight essays by the liberal historian Xu Jilin, translated and edited by David Ownby. To be published by Cambridge University Press in the summer of 2018.
* **[China Dream-Chasers.](/china-dream-chasers/)** A series of eight essays by Chinese intellectuals of a variety of affiliations, all published in the context of China’s rise and President Xi Jinping’s “China Dream” campaign.
* **[Voices from China's Century](/voices-from-chinas-century/)**[.](/) A series of fifteen essays representing a variety of “voices” from the contemporary Chinese intellectual scene, to be published by Columbia University Press in 2019.
* [China and the Post-Pandemic World](/china-and-the-post-pandemic-world/). Ongoing translations of Chinese intellectuals' reactions to the coronavirus pandemic.
